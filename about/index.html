Harrison Gill was born and raised in Chicago.

He's still figuring himself out while, among other curiosities, studying graphic design at Boston University. He loves traveling, modern architecture, A24 films, existentialism, Miles Davis, Frank Ocean, tasteful streetwear, Vagabund Moto, justice, mangos, and dogs. He's a sucker for dense cities who yet eventually wants a farm with his family in the Alps of Switzerland, to which he's never even been.
